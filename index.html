Tracks

#PowerOfVoice

We can do a lot with our voices, sounds and words than just convey. Can we make technology more expressive using the same concepts? Build something on voice assistants or let your code make your next EDM party track, the possibilities here are endless.

#Pathshala

We are leading towards an age of e-learning. Can we move any closer to the usual classroom experience while still sitting behind our screens (or not 😛)?

#VirtuoConnect

Quite relevant to the current scenario where each sector is working towards achieving better connectivity, not just in terms of the internet but also about how we can virtually come closer to reality? Augmented, maybe?


#ThisPandemic

More about rising to transform the situation we are all witnessing, but can you think beyond just the lines of simply playing with the count data various governments are providing?

#LocalVocalFocal

How about we start innovating and build something to make our Life@NU better? Or maybe something that can make student life productive at the same time? Might just end up making you popular on campus 😎

#GoodKarma

Can we build something to give back to society and earn some good karma points?

#Atmanirbhar

The Open Track for those who don’t want to quarantine their amazing ideas in their heads? From blockchain to supply chain, let’s break the chains ⛓
